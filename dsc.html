What is a Digital Signature?

A Digital Signature Certificate (DSC) is a secure digital identity instrument issued by government-approved Certifying Authorities (CCA) to validate the authenticity of electronic documents and digital forms.

Just like a physical hand signature represents authentic individual authorization, an encrypted cryptographic DSC guarantees that electronic files cannot be tampered with or modified once stamped—making your business workflows completely ironclad.
